Soccer

Why I like soccer

My family would always watch and play soccer while I was growing up. This developed my interest in soccer and I still enjoy watching it today even if I don't play it as much.

Favorite Team

My favorite team is Barcelona since my dad supports them and we used to watch all their games while I was growing up. I still watch them play today.

Favorite Player

My favorite player is Lionel Messi because his playstyle is very fun to watch.

Basketball

Why I like basketball

I started playing basketball with my friends in middle school during gym class and found it very fun. I still continue to play and I regularly follow the NBA.

Favorite Team

My favorite team is the Milwaukee Bucks. I originally became a fan of them because of Giannis since he is my favorite player but I continue to be a fan of the team due to their gritty playstyle.

Favorite Player

My favorite player is Giannis Antetokounmpo because he plays with a lot of effort on the court on both the offensive and defensive ends. His work ethic and humility is also very inspirational.

Powerlifting

Why I like powerlifting

I discovered powerlifting through YouTube videos when I first started working out in the gym in the summer before my freshman year of high school. I became fascinated with the sport and since then have followed a powerlifting plan in the gym.

Favorite Powerlifter

My favorite powerlifer is Russel Orhii as he is a very positive and motivating person. I found him through YouTube when I first started powerlifting and found his content to be very useful.

Favorite Lift

My favorite lift in powerlifting is the squat because it is a fun movement to do and my most consistent lift.
